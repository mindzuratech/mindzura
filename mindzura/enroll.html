By submitting this enrollment form, you agree to provide accurate and complete information. Any false information may result in cancellation of your enrollment.

2. Payment Terms

Course fees must be paid in full before the start date unless a payment plan has been approved. Fees are non-refundable after the first week of classes.

3. Attendance Policy

Regular attendance is required. Students missing more than 20% of classes without prior notice may be required to retake the course at additional cost.

4. Code of Conduct

Students are expected to maintain professional behavior. Any form of harassment or disruptive behavior may result in immediate dismissal without refund.

5. Certification

Certificates will be awarded only to students who complete all course requirements and maintain a minimum 70% grade average.

Your personal information will be used solely for enrollment and educational purposes. We will not share your data with third parties without your consent.
